Tips & Guidelines
Required Information

Name, relationship, and primary phone number are required. All other fields help provide better emergency response.

Primary Contact

Designate one person as the primary emergency contact who will be contacted first in case of emergencies.

Multiple Contacts

It's recommended to have at least 2-3 emergency contacts for redundancy and availability.

Keep Updated
  • Update contact information regularly
  • Verify phone numbers periodically
  • Ensure contacts are available 24/7
  • Include backup contacts
